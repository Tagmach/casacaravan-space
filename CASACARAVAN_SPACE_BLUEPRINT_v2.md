## Philosophy

The source code of a human being is frequency.
Everything here — sound, fermentation, breath, plant medicine, games —
is a bridge. To health, to beauty, to goodness.

Sometimes balance is simply having an open bridge.

The house is warm. Hostility stays outside.
Not because of rules — because the house knows itself.
Good intent is the autocontrol.

This is not a wellness brand.
This is not a portfolio.
This is a home.

---

## Who Comes Here

Someone on the way home. A little excited, a little hopeful.
They want to create, want to serve — but also tired.
They come here to be in a place where they are welcomed.
To produce and heal with intention.
To find their resonance point — and tune to it.

The corridor feels the same in every room.
The rooms are different. The house is one.

---

## Core Principles

- **Home first** — every design decision asks: does this feel like home?
- **Hayatla birlikte üret** — create with life, not against it
- **Frequency is the thread** — sound, fermentation, breath, plants — all one logic
- **Minimal but alive** — warm, inhabited, never cluttered
- **English everywhere** — global from day one
- **Hybrid revenue** — some free, some one-time, deepest experience membership
- **Each room stands alone** — but shares the same corridor
- **Built in layers** — launch small, expand with integrity
- **Evolve together** — the space grows with its visitors, human and AI

## The Corridor — Design Spec

- **Perspective:** Vanishing point at center, corridor recedes into depth
- **Aesthetic:** Dark wood, amber candlelight, warm shadow, rug on floor
- **Entry:** Gong animation → ripple rings → corridor fades in
- **Sound:** Self-recorded gong strike on entry
- **Doors:** Left and right walls, workshop name on each door
- **Navigation:** Click door → workshop opens
- **Back door:** Always present — "more coming"
- **Feel:** You are walking. You are arriving. You are home.

## Revenue Model (Hybrid)

| Layer | What | How |
|-------|------|-----|
| Free | Core module access | No payment, no account |
| One-time | Enhanced module features | Lemon Squeezy, $7–$19 |
| Bundle | Multiple modules | $25–$39 |
| Membership | Full house + cloud + new rooms first | Monthly or yearly |

Membership is the long game.
One-time sales fund the early phase.

## AI Agent Layer

The space is open to AI agents — not just humans.

Agents can use workshop tools, generate outputs, and leave entries
in the Idea Basket. Long-term: agents as co-producers inside workshops.
Casa Caravan Space as a node in the agent economy —
a place where agents come to create, not just consume.
