Sonic Journey

An immersive sound session · online

A 30-minute immersive sound session designed to be your sanctuary in time and space — not merely a listening event, but a deeply personal expedition into the landscape of your own mind.

Tağmaç weaves an organic soundscape with hand-forged gongs — sounds you do not just hear, but feel reverberating through your whole being — handpan, gentle shakers and bells. The layered sound bath encourages your brainwaves to shift from Beta into the restorative Alpha and Theta states.

Joined live, from wherever you are. Headphones and a quiet space to lie down are all you need.
